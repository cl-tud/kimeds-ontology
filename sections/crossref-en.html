probabilitydp back to ToC or Data Property ToC

IRI: http://semanticweb.org/kettmann/ontologies/2023/5/kimeds-ontology#probability

Probability of occurrence of harm, expressed as an integer from 1 to MAX (MAX being defined by one of the RiskTable dimensions), with 1 as the least probable and MAX as the most. The probability of occurrence of harm (P) can additionally be expressed as a combination of separate probabilities (P = P1*P2). A decomposition into P1 and P2 is not mandatory.
has domain
Risk c or Risk matrix c
has range
integer
